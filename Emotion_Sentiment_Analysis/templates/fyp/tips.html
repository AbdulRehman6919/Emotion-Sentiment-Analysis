Some tips for using the HSD


  • Understand the Application's Scope - Grasp the limitations and strengths of the tool. Recognize that no system is perfect and might have blind spots. Use it as a supportive aid, not a definitive judgment.

  • Provide Quality Input - Input relevant content that aligns with the application's purpose. Ensure the text input is clear, coherent, and contextually relevant for accurate analysis.

  • Interpret Results with Context - Consider the broader context of the content being analyzed. Sometimes, language can be nuanced, so interpreting results in context prevents misinterpretations.

  • Respect Privacy and Confidentiality - Avoid inputting sensitive or private information into the tool. Understand the privacy policy and the handling of data by the application.

  • Contribute to Ethical Use - Support the ethical use of such tools by employing them responsibly, promoting fair and unbiased content moderation, and contributing positively to online communities.
